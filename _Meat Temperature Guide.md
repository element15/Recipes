# Meat Temperature Guide
Created 11 Mar 2020

## Chicken and Turkey
- 170 ˚F

## Beef and Lamb
Round down to the nearest temperature

- 125 ˚F Rare
- 130 ˚F Medium Rare
- 135 ˚F Medium
- 140 ˚F Medium Well
- 155 ˚F Well Done
- 160 ˚F Ground

## Pork
Round down to the nearest temperature

- 145 ˚F Medium Rare
- 150 ˚F Medium
- 160 ˚F Well Done
- 160 ˚F Ground
